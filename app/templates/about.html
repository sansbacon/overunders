About Over-Under Contests

What is Over-Under Contests?

Over-Under Contests is a platform for creating and participating in Yes/No prediction contests. Whether you're predicting sports outcomes, entertainment events, or any other binary questions, our platform makes it easy to compete with friends and the community.

How It Works
Create

Set up contests with custom Yes/No questions and lock dates.

Participate

Join contests and make your predictions before the lock date.

Compete

See how you rank on the leaderboard after contests lock.

Features
  • Easy contest creation with custom questions
  • Flexible lock dates for predictions
  • Real-time leaderboards and scoring
  • Email authentication for security
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Contest invitations via email
